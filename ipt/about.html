About

Nestled within a luscious green forest cover stretching over a vast expanse of area, the Institute of Printing Technology and Government Polytechnic College was established in 1967 by the Govt. of Kerala under the department of Technical Education and is approved by AICTE (Permanent ID: 1-474656361) and affiliated to SBTE (Affiliation No.: SBTE101901).

Initially conceived as an Institute of Printing Technology, later developed and evolved to be one of the major centres of excellence in Technical Education with the incorporation of the Department of Electronics Engineering in 1987 and the Department of Computer Engineering in 1989 bestowing it a magnificent status. It has the distinct privilege of being the only Institution in the entire state offering a three-year Diploma Programme in Printing Technology.

Surrounded on all sides by historic centres and icons of culture and education such as the Kerala Kalamandalam at Cheruthuruthi, deemed to be University of Art and Culture, the Regional Agricultural Research Station (RARS)- Kerala Agricultural University and the SNGS College at Pattambi, the birth place of the renowned Malayalam poet, performer, satirist and inventor of the local art form of Ottamthullal, Kalakkathu Kunchan Nambiar and the celebrated Nila river, the institution has been a landmark in the academic and technical progress of the region.

It is worthy of mention that the college is singularly proud of its Alumni Association that comprises of many distinguished and illustrious personalities including Vice-chancellors of Universities and enterprising Engineers placed in top-tier companies. An active and vibrant Parent Teacher Association chime in perfectly with the Alma mater guaranteeing a holistic development of the learners. The Skill Development programmes by ASAP (Additional Skill Acquisition Programme, a joint initiative of General and Higher Education Departments, Kerala) that is in close semblance with a Community College and Continuing Education programmes like the CCEK ( Centre for Continuing Education Kerala) ensure that the college pays off rewarding outputs contributing immensely to our community.

The campus is well connected by air, railway and road transport. and can boast of the Shoranur Railway Station, one of the major railway junctions in the state and the Pattambi Railway Station nearby.
